Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December–March and July–August). However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests — just get in touch!
Are your tours fully customisable?
Absolutely. Every Grace Leisure tour is custom-designed from scratch based on your interests, pace, budget and travel dates. We don't offer fixed group tours.
Do you arrange accommodation?
Yes! We can recommend and book accommodation ranging from boutique guesthouses and eco lodges to five-star hotels — tailored to your budget and preferences.
What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?
Sri Lanka can be visited year-round. The west and south coasts are best December–March, while the east coast is ideal May–September. The cultural triangle is good year-round. We'll advise based on your travel dates.
Do I need a visa for Sri Lanka?
Most nationalities require an Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) visa, available online through the official Sri Lanka ETA portal. We can advise on the process during your consultation.
