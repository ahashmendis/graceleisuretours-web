Who We Are

A Passion for Sri Lanka

Operating under Grace Leisure International (PVT) Ltd, we are a premier Sri Lankan travel company dedicated to creating customised, unforgettable tour experiences that go far beyond ordinary sightseeing.

From misty highland tea estates to golden coastal shores and ancient cultural kingdoms — we curate every detail so you can simply live in the moment. Every itinerary is tailor-made, every guide is an expert, and every journey is uniquely yours.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To craft meaningful, personalised travel experiences that connect travellers deeply with Sri Lanka's culture, nature, and people — exceeding expectations at every step of the journey.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most trusted and internationally recognised tour operator — celebrated for creating journeys that inspire, transform and leave lasting positive impacts on travellers and local communities alike.

Our Values

Integrity, passion, sustainability and excellence guide every decision we make — from guide selection to vehicle maintenance to the smallest itinerary detail.

The People Behind Grace Leisure

Meet Our Founders

Between them, our founders bring over 40 years of tourism industry expertise — their passion for Sri Lanka is the foundation of every journey we design.

30+ Years

Ranmal Fernando

Co-Founder & Director

Ranmal is the driving force behind Grace Leisure International (PVT) Ltd. With over three decades of deep immersion in Sri Lanka's tourism industry, his unrivalled knowledge of the island — from its most celebrated landmarks to its hidden gems — shapes every itinerary the company creates.

His passion for crafting truly unforgettable travel experiences, combined with his commitment to warm, personalised service, has earned Grace Leisure a reputation as one of Sri Lanka's most trusted tour operators among international travellers.

Dimithri Perera
