Featured Destination

Sigiriya: The 8th Wonder

Rising dramatically from the jungle floor, the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya is Sri Lanka's most iconic landmark — a truly awe-inspiring feat of ancient engineering and artistry.

The rock's vertical walls were adorned with exquisite frescoes, and its summit once held a sophisticated palace complex with water gardens below. Today it offers breathtaking panoramic views across hundreds of miles of jungle.
